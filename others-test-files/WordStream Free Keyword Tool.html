Why is my ad account disconnected?

Your ad account may become disconnected if the password to the ad account was changed, the user that connected the account no longer has access to it, or you or the ad account owner removed access.

How do I fix my account?

Click the "Fix Connection" button and enter your ad account credentials. After reconnecting your account we recommend running a Get to pull your latest account information into WordStream Advisor.
